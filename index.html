A New Perspective

Earicle is an externally worn gadget that can be used to convert thoughts to text — either “perceived” or “produced” speech. It is a non-invasive, wearable earpiece that allows humans to converse in natural language with other people without any voice —without opening their mouth, and without externally observable movements — simply by articulating words internally.

Earicle allows users to communicate with A.I. as effortlessly as one’s left brain talks to one’s right brain, so that humans can integrate the power of the Internet into their thinking at every level.
